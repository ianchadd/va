Welcome! Here is a brief overview of the study.

What will I have to do?

This study consists of two tasks, which will be explained in detail in a moment, and a survey. The study should take no longer than 20 minutes in total. Your participation and your answers will be kept anonymous.

How much payment will I receive for my participation?

You will be paid {{ participation_fee }} for completing the study.

Additionally, you may receive additional bonus payments based on your decisions and luck. Your entire payment ({{participation_fee}} + whatever additional amount you earn) will be paid to you via the Prolific platform once your responses have been validated.

There will be several Attention Check questions throughout this study meant to test whether you are paying attention as well as your understanding of the intructions. If you fail to correctly complete any of these Attention Check questions, you may not be paid.
