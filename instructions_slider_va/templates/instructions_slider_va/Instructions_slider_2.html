Example 2 :

Now, consider the following puzzle.

There are 2 moves you can choose from: move the 2 to the right or move the 3 up. What move should you make?

Note that the only tiles that are not in the correct order are the 3 and the 6. So, you'll need to move the 3 up.

After moving the 3 up, the puzzle will be identical to the previous example. So you would then move the 6 up and the puzzle will be solved.
